How to create Olive favicon:

1. Obtain Olive image:
http://www.sallybernstein.com/food/single-articles/SPANISH%20OLIVES.jpg

2. Convert 300x290 JPG to 16x16 PNG:
FastStone Photo Resizer

3. Convert PNG to ICO:
http://www.convertico.com/

4. Add favicon to project.

5. Add link in <head>:
<link rel="shortcut icon" href="/olive/images/olive.ico">
